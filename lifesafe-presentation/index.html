LifeSafe

A secure, decentralized healthcare management platform that enhances patient data privacy, transparency, and access control.

Problem Statement

Traditional healthcare systems rely on centralized databases vulnerable to cyberattacks, data manipulation, and limited interoperability. Patients lack full control over who accesses their medical records. LifeSafe addresses this by proposing a blockchain-inspired architecture that enables secure storage, granular access control, and transparency.

Literature Review

Existing blockchain-based healthcare solutions focus on immutability and decentralization but often lack practical usability and scalable access models.

Research Gap

Current systems do not integrate patient-controlled permissions, emergency access protocols, and real-world interface usability in one unified system.

System Methodology

Frontend Layer

React.js based modular interface for patient and record management.

Data Architecture

Structured digital record storage with future Ethereum + IPFS integration.

Security Model

Role-based access control and encryption-ready blockchain architecture.

Results & Analysis

System Efficiency 92%

The LifeSafe prototype demonstrates improved record organization, access transparency, and secure patient data handling compared to traditional centralized systems.
